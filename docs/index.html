About Me

Hi, great seeing you!

My full name is Keith Laurenz Ubag. All throughout my whole life I have always been enthusiastic about gadgets, I loved fiddling with the back of my phone, watching videos regarding technology, and most importantly I always tried to understand how a certain part of it worked. You could say that my understanding of programming stems from my curiousty of such things.

At the age of 10 I started getting into video editing, using simple mobile applications like "Kinemaster" and "Powerdirector". Now at the age of 16, I more or less understand the basics of editing from the effects, the keyframes, basic easing and so on. This has also applied to my visualization of web designing, it helps me stay creative and have a different output from the rest.

As a student I always try to take my time and chill when I do my school works, one of my favourite things to do while doing my school works is listening to music. It helps me stay awake, and keeps my attention on what I'm doing (Unless I'm singing along to it LMAO). In the future, I hope to continue exploring my passion for technology and creative work, and to use my skills to make a positive impact on the world. Whether that's through developing new software, creating engaging content, or something else entirely, I am excited to see where my life takes me.

If you are student, or someone interested in learning more about me or my work, please don't hesitate to reach out. I am always happy to connect with like-minded individuals and explore new opportunities for collaboration.
